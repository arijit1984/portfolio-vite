# Case Study — Monolith Re-architecture

**Role:** Senior Software Engineer
**Timeline:** May 2019 – Nov 2020

## Overview
Re-architected a legacy monolithic application into modular Spring Boot microservices enabling faster development and simplified operations.

## Responsibilities
- Decomposed monolith into 5 modular services with well-defined APIs and database segregation.
- Introduced CI/CD, containerization and environment parity across dev/stage/prod.

## Outcome
- Reduced bug resolution time by 35% and onboarding time for new developers from 2 weeks to 5 days.
